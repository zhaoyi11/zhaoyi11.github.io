I am a first-year PhD student at Robot Learning Group, Aalto University, advised by Prof. [Joni Pajarinen](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=-2fJStwAAAAJ&hl=zh-CN) and Prof. [Juho Kannala](https://users.aalto.fi/~kannalj1/). My research interests lie in the application of reinforcement learing for robotics. I am currently working on offline reinforcement learning and model-based reinforcement learning.

I get my MSc degree from Aalto University with a major in Control, Robotics, and Autonomous. My master thesis was "Model-based Reinforcement Learning from Pixels", supervised by Prof. Juho Kannala.
